The Cruise Technology school was founded in 1998 by our director Simõn Fuller.
Simõn Fuller graduated from the best schools in New York and when he graduated he
decided to create a school that had enough resources to prepare future
young people for the world of work. He did research on what is most requested in the world and founded Cruise Technology,
with the aim of not only training students but also making them experience spectacular things in the world of technology.

We currently have more than 20 schools open around the world,

We currently have laboratory rooms in this unit (with the best computers available today),
computer maintenance rooms, language rooms and other courses offered.
All with air conditioning and specialized and trained teachers with excellence.
all with the aim of helping to form young people and prepare them for life.
